One of my favorite marathon moments of all time was in 2022 when Eliud Kipchoge ran a time of 1:59:40. This moment did not count for an official World Record, because he was assisted by pacers and other technology not permitted in world record legal competitions. Despite this, INEOS held a challenge for Kipchoge to prove to the world that it was humanly possible to break 2 hours in the marathon.
